The Project Hub

The Project Hub is the main starting point for configuring your project.

Figure 1. Project Hub

From the Project Hub, you can access all settings and information that are relevant for your data donation project. It consists of four main areas:

  1. Project Details: General settings for your project.

  2. Project Configuration: Here, you can configure each step that your participants will go through when taking part in your project.

  3. Data Center: Here, you can download the collected data, access your project’s logs, and find some general participation statistics.

  4. Danger Zone: Here, you find options to delete the project, the project’s data, or the data belonging to specific participants.
